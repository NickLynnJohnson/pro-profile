About Me

Avid learner and team player

Hi! My name is Nick Johnson and I am a Product Manager with startup experience and technical programming skills. I like creating new value for companies and also resolving pain points, bottlenecks, or inefficiencies that a company might have.

The startup that I worked at is called BioIQ. It provided a solid foundation for me for how a company grows and needs to pivot. As it's tenth employee, it was definitely a fun, whirlwind experience to see it expand to 100 employees in only six years and become profitable at that.

I left BioIQ however because I was itching for some more specific training to focus on instead of the "informal training" that I had been teaching myself and getting from the environment around me. My goal was to get more training in the following, and so far I have completed the first one - "Website Development/Programming":

  • Website Development/Programming
  • Product Management
  • Project Management
  • Process Management

I should explain that though I have programming skills, I do not consider myself a full-time engineer. Instead, I prefer to use my newly acquired skills to become a better product manager who can more easily create products and work with other engineers in the process. I also consider Product Management as the pinnacle role which then utilizes project and process management to accomplish its goals. Project and process management are life-long skills that I want to constantly iterate on and get better at.

Aside from work-related things, I have also started learning Mandarin. This too will be a life-long process, but I find Chinese/Taiwanese culture to be very interesting and since I can't roll my "r" in Spanish anyway (but seem to be okay at tones), it's now time to finally be able to speak something besides English ;). I highly recommend by the way the small, personalized, startup language school I found for learning Chinese: Excel Mandarin. Their teachers are amazing, the curriculum is super engaging and self-curated (no textbook), and they create a very cool learning community with other students. Go check 'em out...

In closing, hopefully this provides a decent snap shot into the range of skills and aptitudes I possess, and what working with me could be like. I appreciate your time in reading my bio and look forward to talking to you soon. Please feel free to contact me by navigating to my website's Contact Page or you can look into my background more by visiting my Resume Page.

Cheers,

- Nick

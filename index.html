My Take on Delivering Quality

Hi there :) and thank you for visiting my page. This may sound counter-intuitive but my personal goal is to be so good at what I do, whether in product, project, or process, that the recipient isn't even aware of how well their experience was. It's like having good table manners: no one should praise you for how good you were at using your silverware or not having your elbows on the table...instead they should simply have enjoyed spending a meal with you and want to do it again sometime soon.
